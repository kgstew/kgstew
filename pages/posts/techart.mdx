## A monthly gathering of technical artists

I started this group with a friend to create a regular gathering of technical artists in the area. When I moved to Denver I found it difficult to connect with the maker/builder community through existing events. I wanted to create a place for people to get together a talk shop with other makers on the projects they are working on and find opportunities for collaboration.
